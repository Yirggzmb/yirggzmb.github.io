Custom Designs - Wild World

Here are a selection of custom designs created in Wild World. The only way to get them is to recreate them yourself in game. These may also be useful in other games, however the color palettes there are different and you may have to tinker. Click on any image to see it larger.

Each design will have, at minimum, a screenshot of the design in the designer with and without the grid. There will also likely be at least one image with the design using an alternative palette, so as to make the specific colors more clear which is used where. Where possible, I've also added screenshots or photos of the designs used in game, some in emulator and others photos of my actual DS.

Wooden Planks

  • Palette: 5
  • Notes: Includes a variant with artwork on the planks, meant for sprinkling in with the main path

Stone Bricks

  • Palette: 15
  • Notes: Looks pretty good in several palettes

Cobblestones

  • Palette: 15
  • Notes: Meant to resemble the cobblestone around buildings, even if it doesn't match perfectly

Blue Tiles

  • Palette: 12
  • Notes: Based loosely on the dirt pattern found here just with a bunch of stuff drawn over it. Replace dirt background with something else to use any palette you want.
